Methodology

transplant.today uses a multi-factor algorithm that analyzes 40+ data points across 8 major categories to generate a relative location suitability score for each of the 248 US transplant programs.

Not clinically validated. Scores are relative comparisons based on publicly available SRTR/UNOS data. They are not individual clinical assessments and should not guide medical decisions. Consult a transplant team.

What simulation cannot account for: HLA typing and crossmatch compatibility, specific disease etiology and comorbidities, current functional status, individual OPO performance, or your relationship with specific transplant programs.
