What TransplantMatch Does

Multi-Factor Scoring

8 weighted categories analyze 40+ data points to generate relative suitability scores for your specific organ transplant needs.

Wait Time Forecasting

1,000-iteration Monte Carlo simulation models transplant probability over time with competing risks of mortality and waitlist removal.

Equity Analysis

48-profile demographic matrix evaluates fairness across blood types, age brackets, and sex. Gini coefficient measures per-city equality.

Interactive Map

Explore 248 transplant centers with 10 data overlays including health demographics, donor registration, and spatial heatmaps.

What-If Scenarios

Adjust donor rates and wait time multipliers to see how UNOS policy changes would affect transplant probabilities via real-time re-simulation.

Export & Reports

Download results as PDF, CSV, or JSON. Export charts as PNG images. Share results via URL parameters.

Disclaimer

This tool is for educational and exploratory purposes only. TransplantMatch is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and cannot predict individual transplant outcomes.

Scores are relative comparisons based on publicly available regional statistics. They are not clinical assessments.

Always consult your transplant team and healthcare providers before making any decisions about where to list for a transplant.
