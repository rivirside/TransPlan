Data Sources

All data is sourced from US federal agencies and updated regularly. Click any card to visit the official source.

SRTR

Scientific Registry of Transplant Recipients

Center-level transplant program data including wait times, volumes, and post-transplant outcomes for all 248 US programs.

Updated semi-annually → OPTN

Organ Procurement & Transplantation Network

National transplant data including organ allocation policies, waiting list statistics, and donor information.

Updated monthly → CDC

Centers for Disease Control & Prevention

County-level health demographics: diabetes, obesity, hypertension, and smoking prevalence from the PLACES dataset.

Updated annually → CMS

Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services

Hospital quality ratings, patient safety indicators, and transplant program reputation scores.

Updated quarterly → EPA

Environmental Protection Agency

Air quality monitoring station data including AQI readings, PM2.5, ozone, and pollutant levels across the US.

Updated daily → NHTSA

National Highway Traffic Safety Administration

State-level traffic fatality rates used to model traumatic organ donor supply patterns.

Updated annually → HRSA

Health Resources & Services Administration

State-level organ donor registration rates and Donate Life America participation data.

Updated annually → BLS

Bureau of Labor Statistics

Regional cost of living indices and employment data used for socioeconomic support scoring.
